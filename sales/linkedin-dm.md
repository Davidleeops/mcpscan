# LinkedIn DM Variants

Use LinkedIn for light first touches and follow-up after a connection is accepted. Do not paste long audit details unless the prospect asks for scope.

## Variant 1

Hi {{first_name}}, noticed you are working around AI agents/dev tools. Quick question: have you reviewed what your MCP servers can access yet? We run fixed-scope MCP audits that map servers, tools, permissions, secret exposure, and prompt-injection risk. Useful before enterprise customers ask.

## Variant 2

Hi {{first_name}}, MCP is getting connected to code, tickets, databases, and internal APIs very quickly. We help teams answer one practical question: "What can the agent access, and what should we fix first?" Open to me sending the audit scope?

## Variant 3

Hi {{first_name}}, if {{company}} is using MCP with real credentials, we offer a 3-5 day security audit: inventory, risky permissions, exposed secrets, and remediation checklist. No SaaS setup required. Want the sample scope?

## Connection Note

Hi {{first_name}}, saw your work around {{trigger}}. I am researching how teams are reviewing MCP/agent access before customer or internal rollout. Would be glad to connect.

## After Connection Accepted

Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for connecting. The short version: we help teams using MCP answer "what can this agent actually access, and what should we fix first?" If {{company}} is using MCP with real credentials, I can send the one-page audit scope.

## Follow-Up After 4 Business Days

Quick follow-up. A good fit is usually a team connecting MCP to code, tickets, databases, CRM, cloud, or internal APIs. If that is on your roadmap, I can send the sample scope.

## Positive Reply

Great. To confirm fit before I send the payment link: roughly how many MCP servers are in scope, and are they connected to code, tickets, databases, customer data, cloud, CRM, or internal APIs?
