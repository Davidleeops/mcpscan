Use this after a prospect replies. The goal is to map scope to the right audit package and move the account toward paid intake.

Approval Rule

Do not send follow-up notes, checkout links, or scope summaries until the exact recipient and exact final content are approved in the same turn.

Open With This

The useful version of this conversation is practical: what MCP access are you trying to enable, what would make AppSec comfortable, and what evidence do you need before rollout?

Core Questions

  1. Which MCP-enabled tools are in scope?
  2. How many MCP servers/configs are involved?
  3. Are any tools write-capable, destructive, admin-level, filesystem-level, database-connected, or network-connected?
  4. Where do configs live?
  5. What credentials/auth patterns are involved?
  6. What decision do you need: approve, approve with guardrails, or block?
  7. Who needs the final report?
  8. Can you provide sanitized configs first?
