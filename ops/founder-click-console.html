Decision

Buy the .com if it is standard price. Security buyers trust boring domains. The cheap extension can be used as a fallback or parked redirect, but it should not be the primary if the .com is available.

Positioning

Sell this as an independent MCP readiness and evidence audit. Do not lead with "AI governance platform" or "MCP gateway." Those categories are crowded and invite enterprise-platform comparisons too early.

Known Costs

Spaceship currently advertises .com at about $8.88 for year one and $9.98 renewal. Spacemail Pro is advertised at $14.16 for two years after a 30-day trial. .online can be $0.98 for year one, but renews much higher and is weaker for trust.
